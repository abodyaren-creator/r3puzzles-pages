❓ FAQ

How do I change the language?

From the Settings screen (gear icon on the home screen).

Is my data saved?

Yes — all your progress and scores are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling clears them, so don't uninstall unless you want a fresh start.

How do I get more hints in Solitaire and Parking Puzzle?

You start with 3 free hints per game. When they run out, watch a short ad to earn 5 more hints.

Can I remove the ads?

Ads help us keep the app free. An ad-free option may come in future updates.

How do I delete my data?

See the Data Deletion Request page.

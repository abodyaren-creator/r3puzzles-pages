Last updated: May 1, 2026

Welcome to 3R Puzzles Games — a collection of puzzle games. We fully respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

✓ Our app does not collect any personal data from users.

The app saves the following data on your device only (never leaves your device):

The app does not request any sensitive permissions from Android or iOS.

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any data, the app is completely safe for children under 13 and complies with COPPA.
